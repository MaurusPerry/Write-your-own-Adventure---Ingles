The animals, tired of Jones' constant abuse, decide to rebel. Without planning, they launch an attack and surround their tormentors. The situation became chaotic; The men, terrified by the unexpected bravery of the animals, flee in terror down the cart path, pursued by the jubilant cries of their former slaves.

From the window, Mrs. Jones watches the commotion and, in a panic, hastily packs her belongings before escaping, followed by Moisés. Meanwhile, the animals close the farm gate with a roar that marks the end of human rule. Joy fills their hearts, and for a few moments they can barely believe in their new freedom.

Excitedly, they gallop around the edge of the farm, making sure no hidden humans remain. They return to the main building and begin destroying the symbols of their oppression. They break down the tack room door and throw chains, knives and other instruments of torture into the well. Each object that disappears represents a step towards your freedom.
