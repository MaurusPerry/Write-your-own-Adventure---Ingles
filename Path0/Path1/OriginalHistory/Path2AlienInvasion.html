The animals had heard legends about Slenderman, a mysterious being described as tall and thin, pale and faceless, who wore an elegant suit. Believing that his power could guide them in their new life, they decided to name the farm "Slender Farm" in his honor.

With the new name, the animals began to worship Slenderman, performing rituals in his honor, hoping to attract his attention and blessing. One night, under the light of the full moon, they held a special ceremony. As they danced and sang, in a flash of shadows, Slenderman materialized before them. The amazed animals were attracted to his aura, and Slenderman, recognizing their devotion, promised them assistance.

With a gesture of his hand, he called to beings from another world, extraterrestrials who arrived in glowing ships. The aliens, fascinated by the animals' rebellion and their desire to build a great future, decided to help them. Together, they began to erect monumental pyramids and extraordinary structures, joining forces to create a legacy that would endure for millennia.

Thousands of years later, when animals left Earth to explore new worlds, their legacy was imprinted in the pyramids and enigmatic markings on the ground known as the Nazca Lines.

When humanity re-emerged, they found these ancient monuments and, without understanding their true origin, began to venerate the animals that had left their mark on Earth. The Egyptians, in particular, honored cats and other creatures, believing them to be sacred beings who had built these wonders with the help of their extraterrestrial friends. Although after several years, this devotion to animals began to disappear, and they once again considered themselves pets of humans.

Thus, in this story, humanity inherited a world that had been forged by the union of animals and aliens, a legacy of greatness and mystery that continues to fascinate future generations.
